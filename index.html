The Problem

Predator losses cost small and mid‑sized farms thousands of dollars annually. Night‑time incursions are hard to monitor, and existing solutions (trail cams, manual patrols) are reactive, labor‑intensive, and miss critical moments.

  • Livestock predation (e.g., kids/calves) spikes at night and during birthing.
  • Producers lack timely alerts and actionable context to respond.
  • Most camera systems aren’t trained for wildlife identification.

Our Solution

Install rugged cameras + edge AI to run species‑specific detectors. Night Vyper sends instant alerts with annotated snapshots, confidence, and location—so owners can act (lights, sirens, human response).

  • Edge first (Jetson) with optional cloud dashboard.
  • Species packs: Coyote, Bobcat, Fox; extensible to regional predators.
  • Simple pricing: hardware kit + subscription for monitoring & updates.

Why Now

Edge AI is finally practical for 24/7 outdoor use. Livestock operations seek automation that pays for itself with a single prevented incident per season.

  • Affordable edge GPUs (Jetson Orin Nano class).
  • Improved low‑light cameras & IR illumination.
  • Farm Wi‑Fi/PoE infrastructure maturing.
